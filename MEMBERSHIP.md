# Membership

## Membership lifecycle

1. Application or invitation.
2. Identity and role verification.
3. Acceptance of the Code of Conduct and security rules.
4. Assignment to `asr-contributors` with limited access.
5. A documented onboarding task and named sponsor.
6. Review after approximately 30 days.
7. Assignment to one or more permanent teams.

## Access principles

- Least privilege by default.
- Access is granted through teams rather than directly whenever possible.
- Sensitive repositories require a specific operational need.
- Departing members must be removed promptly and their credentials rotated when relevant.
- Organization owners remain a very small group.
